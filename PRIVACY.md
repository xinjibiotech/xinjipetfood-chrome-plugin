# Privacy Policy

Xinji Pet B2B Product Link is a local Chrome extension for product reference, official website navigation, product comparison, export and favorites.

## Data Collection

The extension does not collect, transmit, sell or share personal data.

## Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome local storage only to save product favorites on the user's own browser. Favorite data is not sent to Xinji Pet Food, third-party servers or analytics tools.

## Website Links

When a user clicks an official website link, Chrome opens the selected page on `https://www.xinjipetfood.com/`. The destination website may have its own privacy policy.

## Exports

CSV and JSON files are generated locally in the browser from product data shown in the extension.

## Contact

For questions about this extension, contact the project maintainer or the official website team through [https://www.xinjipetfood.com/contact/](https://www.xinjipetfood.com/contact/).
